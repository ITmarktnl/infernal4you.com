Privacy Policy – Infernal-Capers.com

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us at Infernal-Capers.com. We are dedicated to protecting your personal data and ensuring its confidentiality. This Privacy Policy outlines how we gather, utilize, share, and secure your information when you access our website.

1. Data Collection

When you engage with our services, we may obtain the following personal information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Old School RuneScape Account Name (required to fulfill our services)

Please note: We never ask for or collect your Old School RuneScape account password. Your password remains confidential and is never shared with us.

Additionally, we may collect non-identifiable information, including:

  • Browser Details and Version
  • Operating System Information
  • IP Address
  • Device Specifications
  • Site Interaction Metrics (e.g., pages viewed, duration of visits)
  • Cookies

To improve your browsing experience and monitor site usage, Infernal-Capers.com employs cookies. You have the option to disable cookies through your browser settings; however, certain website functionalities may be limited without them.

2. Purpose of Data Usage

Infernal-Capers.com utilizes the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery and Management: To provide the services you purchase, including accessing your Old School RuneScape account name when necessary.
  • Communication: To send you updates about your orders, promotional offers, and any changes to our services.
  • Service Enhancement: To analyze non-personal data to understand user behavior and improve our offerings.
  • Legal Compliance: To use your information as mandated by applicable laws, regulations, or legal procedures.

3. Information Sharing Practices

We do not sell or rent your personal information to external entities. Infernal-Capers.com may disclose your information under specific circumstances, such as:

  • Third-Party Service Providers: Sharing information with trusted partners who assist in operating our website and delivering services.
  • Legal Obligations: Disclosing your data when required by law or in response to valid requests from governmental authorities.
  • Protection of Rights: Sharing information to safeguard the rights, property, or safety of Infernal-Capers.com, our users, or others.

Importantly, since we do not collect passwords, there is no risk of password disclosure through our data-sharing practices.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Despite our efforts, no method of data transmission or electronic storage is entirely secure. While we aim to use commercially acceptable means to safeguard your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Rest assured, as we do not collect passwords, your account security remains solely under your control.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites or services that are not operated by Infernal-Capers.com. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any external websites you visit.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You are entitled to certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access and Retrieve Your Data: Request a copy of your personal information.
  • Update or Correct Information: Ask for corrections or updates to your data.
  • Delete Your Data: Request the removal of your personal information from our records.
  • Restrict Data Processing: Object to or limit the processing of your personal information.

To exercise these rights, please reach out to us via our Live Chat feature.

7. Policy Modifications

Infernal-Capers.com retains the authority to revise or amend this Privacy Policy at any time. Significant changes will be communicated through our website or via email. Continued use of our website following any modifications signifies your acceptance of the updated terms.
